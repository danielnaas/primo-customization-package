How does this work?

NEW: InfoKat Research Assistant: A generative AI-powered tool that enables natural language queries of our academic content. It will recommend five sources and distill the content of the descriptions and abstracts. We encourage you to give it a try!

InfoKat Discovery finds items from the University of Kentucky Collections along with many electronic resources, such as peer-reviewed sources, journal and newspaper articles, book chapters, books, reviews, videos, legal documents and much more. This search includes content from many, though not all, of the individual databases provided by the UK Libraries.

Sign-in in order to:
  • Renew books and videos
  • Request holds on library materials
  • Create favorites lists and save search queries
  • Export citations to Endnote Web and other formats
