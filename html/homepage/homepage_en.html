InfoKat Discovery - July 2021 Update

What's new?

  • Enhanced Journal Search Interface: Use the top navigation bar "journals" link to access a combined print and electronic journal search interface. One can now browse by category on the left side of the updated interface.
  • Search Within a Journal: Every journal record now contains a shortcut to start a search inside the contents.
  • Faster Updates: new physical items and electronic resources will appear within minutes after we catalog them instead of a nightly update cycle.
  • URL Change: While one can still reach InfoKat Discovery through the UK Libraries homepage as usual, the direct link has changed (see note below)
NOTE: Any permalinks to InfoKat Discovery records created before July 1, 2021, will stop working at the end of September 2021. We apologize for the inconvenience and can offer a tool to easily convert links to the new style: link to tool. One can also repeat a search in the catalog after July 1 to find a new link.

What is InfoKat Discovery?

InfoKat Discovery finds items from the University of Kentucky Collections along with many electronic resources, such as journal and newspaper articles, book chapters, books, reviews, videos, legal documents and much more. This search includes content from many, though not all, of the individual databases provided by the UK Libraries.

Sign-in in order to:
  • Renew books and videos
  • Create favorites lists
  • Export citations to Endnote Web and other formats
  • View full search results. (Some databases only show results when you're signed in.)
